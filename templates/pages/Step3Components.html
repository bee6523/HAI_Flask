How to use modulation tools?
This is region picker. You can drag and choose where you want AI to refer to when repainting the image. You can then use rectangle and brush to paint on the color map.
This is rectangle tool. You can paint in rectangular shape on the masked region.
This is brush tool. You can choose the brush size and paint in free form on the masked region.
    • What does the color mean?
    Through the colors on the image's masked region, you can see where the AI referred to when repainting the image. Image pixels are represented as the color on same relative position of this color map.
